Visit China

Our standard China Tour package is designed to offer you the best travel experience during your visits to Shanghai, Guangzhou, or Beijing. Travel to other cities is possible with additional costs. The package includes all necessary services such as visa processing, round-trip flight tickets, travel insurance, a 6-night stay at a comfortable 4-star hotel with complimentary breakfast, skilled tour guides and translators, and reliable ground transportation services.

  • How to Apply?
    • To apply for tourist or business visas, all you need to do is provide a copy of your passport and a photo for registration. We will take care of the remaining paperwork and schedule your visa application date to ensure a smooth and efficient process.
