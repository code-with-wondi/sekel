Study in INDIA

Here at our agency Line Addis, we provide comprehensive support in obtaining both tourist and business visas to Schengen States. with our team of highly experienced Travel consultants, you can leave all the details in our capable hands.

  • Our range of services include:
    • Consultation with experts
    • Fast-track admission to universities
    • Assistance with scholarship applications
    • Preparation, booking, and payment for English proficiency exams
    • Help with embassy visits, appointment bookings, and visa document preparation
    • Efficient training for visa interviews, motivation letter, and essay preparation
    • Support for appealing Negative visa decisions
    • Assistance with booking accommodations
    • Pre- departure training and follow up
  • Application documents
    • For undergraduate applications, the required documents are:
    • Valid passport
    • Duly accomplished admission application form
    • Legalized academic transcripts
    • Legalized 12th standard pass in Science from a recognized board of education.
    • Curriculum vitae or comprehensive resume
    • Language proficiency test scores
    • Letter of Motivation or Statement of Purpose
    • Proof of financial capability (bank statements for self-financed students or a letter of sponsorship for students with scholarships)
    For masters applications, the required documents are:
    • A legalized bachelor’s degree with 60% or more marks
    • A legalized academic transcript of the undergraduate degree
    • Language proficiency
    • CV/ Resume
    • Two recommendation letters or references
    • Statement of purpose or motivation letter
  • Application process steps
    • Research and choose a course and university in India.
    • Check eligibility requirements and gather necessary documents.
    • Prepare for entrance exams if required.
    • Apply directly to the universities or through the Study in India portal.
    • Wait for admission decisions and receive offer letters.
    • Apply for a student visa (S1 Visa) at the Indian embassy.
    • Plan your travel and arrival in India.
    • Attend orientation, enroll in courses, and arrange accommodation.
    • Commence your studies in India.
  • Financial Document Requirements
    • The sponsor is required to provide a bank statement with a minimum closing balance of Bank statement of 2000 USD
  • English Requirements
    • IELTS: Overall score of 6
    • TOEFL: Overall score of 60 to 90
    • PTE: Overall score of 50 to 64
    • Duolingo - Overall score of 90+
    • English proficiency Letter from MOE
  • Scholarships
    • ICCR
    • Study in India
  • Intakes
    • January intake, Class starting from July
  • Costs
    • Admission fee – 80-120 USD
    • Tuition fee/year – 5000 – 8000 USD
    • Dormitory/year – 50,000 – 70,000 INR
    • Flight ticket – 19,000 – 23,000 ETB
    • Visa Cost – 10,400 ETB
