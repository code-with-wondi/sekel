Study in South Korea

Here at our agency Line Addis, we provide comprehensive support in obtaining both tourist and business visas to Schengen States. with our team of highly experienced Travel consultants, you can leave all the details in our capable hands.

  • Our range of services include:
    • Consultation with Experts Quick admission to Universities
    • Scholarship applications
    • English proficiency Exam preparation
    • Embassy visit/ Appointment bookings Visa documents preparation and assistance
    • Efficient Visa interview Training Motivation letter and essays preparation
    • Appeal against Visa decisions
    • Pre departure training and follow up
  • Application documents
    • For undergraduate applications, the required documents are:
    • A scan of your diploma (high school > 60% marks)
    • A transcript/record of your previous courses
    • A scan of your passport and/or birth certificate
    • Your CV
    • Testing scores
    • Evidence of scholarship or funding
    • Letters of recommendation (between 2-3)
    • Portfolio and/or writing samples
    For masters applications, the required documents are:
    • A scan of your diploma (Bachelor’s degree – GPA> 2.7)
    • A transcript/record of your previous courses
    • A scan of your passport and/or birth certificate
    • Your CV
    • Testing scores
    • Study Plan
    • Evidence of scholarship or funding
    • Letters of recommendation (between 2-3)
    • Portfolio and/or writing samples
  • Application process steps
    1. Research and choose a course and university in South Korea.
    2. Check eligibility requirements and gather necessary documents.
    3. Prepare for entrance exams or Interview.
    4. Apply directly to the universities or through the University portal.
    5. Apply for the school and government scholarships if it’s available.
    6. Wait for admission decisions and receive offer letters.
    7. Apply for a student visa (D2 Visa) at the S. Korean embassy.
    8. Plan your travel and arrival in Korea.
    9. Attend orientation, enroll in courses, and arrange accommodation.
    10. Commence your studies.
