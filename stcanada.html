Study in CANADA

Here at our agency Line Addis, we provide comprehensive support in obtaining both tourist and business visas to Schengen States. with our team of highly experienced Travel consultants, you can leave all the details in our capable hands.

  • Our range of services include:
    • Consultation with experts
    • Fast-track admission to universities
    • Assistance with scholarship applications
    • Preparation, booking, and payment for English proficiency exams
    • Help with embassy visits, appointment bookings, and visa document preparation
    • Efficient training for visa interviews, motivation letter, and essay preparation
    • Support for appealing Negative visa decisions
    • Assistance with booking accommodations
    • Pre- departure training and follow up
  • Application documents
    • For undergraduate applications, the required documents are:
    • Valid passport
    • High school transcript for grades 9-12
    • Grade 10 and 12 national exam results
    • Recommendation letter
    • Results from English language proficiency exams
    • Essay
    For masters applications, the required documents are:
    • Valid passport
    • Degree certificate and bachelor's transcript/student copy
    • Bachelor transcript/student copy
    • Recommendation letter
    • Resume
  • The application process consists of the following steps:
    • Step 1. Document submission and registration
    • Step 2. Advance service deposit
    • Step 3. University application and payment of application fee
    • Step 4 Conditional interview or exam with the university
    • Step 5 Issuance of invitation and admission documents
    • Step 6 Preparation of visa application documents
    • Step 7 Second service deposit
    • Step 8 Visa Application
    • Step 9 Original Documents submission
    • Step 10. Biometrics scan and Medical diagnosis
    • Step 11. Visa issuance
    • Step 12 Travelling to Canada
  • Financial Document Requirements
    • Living expense (mini 10,000 CAD)
    • Tuition payment confirmation or same amount deposited in bank account
    • In total bank statement of 2-3 million ETB and more is required
    • Affidavit of family/relative
