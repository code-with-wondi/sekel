  • Application documents
    • To apply for a Tourist or business visas, all you need to do is bring your passport and the required supporting documents that demonstrate your socioeconomic ties in Ethiopia. These documents may include bank statements, property ownership certificates, marriage certificates, business ownership or employment certificates, and more. We only require scanned copies or copies of your documents for the application.

We will also schedule your visa application date to ensure a smooth process.

    • Please note that the visa processing time can vary depending on the destination you wish to travel, usually it takes 2 weeks to get visa results once visa application is submitted to the embassy.
