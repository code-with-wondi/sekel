VISIT INDIA

Line Addis warmly invites you to immerse yourself in the vibrant heart of India. We are dedicated to ensuring your voyage to this ancient land is as majestic as India itself.

Inorder to process your visa to India, we need your passport copy and 6 months bank statement.
  • Must visit sites in India
    • Taj Mahal, Agra : Located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, this white marble mausoleum stands as an eternal symbol of love. the Taj Mahal is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New Seven Wonders of the World.
    • Jaipur, Rajasthan: Known as the 'Pink City' for its distinctive terracotta-colored buildings, Jaipur is part of the famous Golden Triangle tourist circuit.
    • Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh: One of the world's oldest inhabited cities, Varanasi, is a city of great religious significance for Hindus. the city is known for its spiritual ambience, historical ghats, temples, and the ritual of Ganga Aarti.
