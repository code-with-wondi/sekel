  • Application documents
    • For undergraduate applications, the required documents are:
    • Passport, which is valid for at least 15 months
    • Grade 9-12 High school transcript (Total sum average above 70%)
    • Legalized University entrance examination/SSLE/Grade 12 matriculation results and must have a pass mark on the exam
    • Legalized Grade 10 Matriculations (if have)
    • Application form
    • English proficiency letter from the Ministry of Education (optional)
    • Eligibility statement: - a statement from MOE declaring that you have a pass mark on your University entrance examination and you are free from cost-sharing payment duties.
    For masters applications, the required documents are:
    • Passport, which is valid for at least 15 months
    • Undergraduate grade report/student copy
    • Legalized Original Degree certificate: CGPA must be above 2.6
    • Application form
    • Eligibility statement from MOE: a letter declaring you are free from cost-sharing duties and that you are eligible to study Masters level studies in private and public universities of Ethiopia
    • Recommendation letter
    • Photo
